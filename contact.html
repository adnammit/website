about

I am currently seeking employment -- if you think I could be a good addition to your team, drop me a line!

A recent graduate of Portland State University, I am an enthusiastic novice programmer. Whether it's fine-tuning visual design with CSS, developing complex object-oriented schemas with C++ or using Javascript to contribute to the rich website responsiveness that web users demand, I am driven by the excitement of learning new skills and solving problems. I am also inordinately obsessed with finding fun ipsums; the Tina ipsum is my favorite so far.

When I'm not writing code I can be found hiking small mountains, rowing on the Willamette River, biking hidden dirt roads, and occassionally taking breaks to crochet and make kimchee.

See you out there!
